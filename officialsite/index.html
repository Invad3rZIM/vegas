Why Limelight?

Almost 1 year ago, I started itching to make music. For a casual hobby, I found the buyin was relatively steep - a good microphone can range hundreds, and so can a DAW

There is an integration requirement too - for the longest time my friends and I were carrying around either paper note-pads or random text-widgets on your phone

I built Limelight to ease the Rap buy-in for amateur musicians

I also believe that music relates human unlike any other medium, and I wanted to offer the framework for a community for my app users
