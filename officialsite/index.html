Why ElectroFlow?

The hippies were right! Music brings us together, it's a surefire way to create, especially with other artist. I firmly believe that music relates human unlike any other medium, and I wanted to offer the framework for a community for my app users

Almost 1 year ago, I started itching to make music. For a casual hobby, I found the buyin was relatively steep - a good microphone can range hundreds, and so can a DAW

There is an integration requirement too - for the longest time my friends and I were carrying around either paper note-pads or random text-widgets on your phone

Everyone who's tried to make it in the music industries knows how saturated it is- I built ElectroFlow to ease the Rap buy-in for amateur musicians and offer a voice for your voice
